Hello!
Welcome to FoodShare.

With FoodShare, we hope to reduce food waste by reallocating it to those who need it the most.

Do you have groceries in the fridge that you don't plan on using?
Leftovers from a large family gathering?
Maybe you own a restaurant, and you have leftover food at the end of the night?

It's simple. Fill out your contact information in the form, include details about the food you are
sharing, and submit. Your food will be added to a list viewable by local food banks and soup
kitchens, who will arrange to pick it up and redistribute it to those in need.
